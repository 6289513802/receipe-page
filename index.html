Chicken Chilli Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken, diced

  2. 1 cup bell peppers, thinly sliced

  3. 1 cup onion, chopped

  4. 3 cloves garlic, minced

  5. 2 tablespoons soy sauce

  6. 1 tablespoon cornstarch

  7. 1 teaspoon ginger, grated

  8. 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes

  9. Salt and pepper to taste

  10. 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix soy sauce, cornstarch, ginger, red chili flakes, salt, and pepper.

  2. Marinate diced chicken in the mixture for 30 minutes.

  3. Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat.

  4. Saut√© garlic until fragrant, then add marinated chicken and cook until browned.

  5. Add sliced bell peppers and chopped onions, stir-fry until vegetables are tender.

  6. Adjust seasoning if needed and serve hot.

Enjoy your delicious Chicken Chilli!
